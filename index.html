The Craft

Wild by Nature.
Permanent by Design.

Every HOLY BUCK piece starts in the field — a shed antler found naturally on the forest floor, left behind by the animal itself.


Nothing is hunted. Nothing is harvested.


Each antler is cleaned, detailed, and cast using the ancient sand casting method. The result: a piece that carries the organic texture and character of the original — made permanent in solid metal.
